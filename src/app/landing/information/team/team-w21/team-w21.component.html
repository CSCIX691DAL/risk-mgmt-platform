The newly improved Risk Profile Page contains the same information from the Semester of Fall 2020; however, the design and layout was adjusted to increase its readability while also improving accessibility. Additionally, the back-end of the Risk Profile Page has changed dramatically, calling all of its data from a database and storing these changes for the user.
