Risk Map Report

What is Risk Map Report? The Risk Map Report is a visual representation of the plausible Risk Profiles defined in *ORGANIZATION NAME*. Each points in the report are influenced by the impact (x-axis) and likelihood (y-axis) assigned in each Risk Profiles plotted in the chart above. The impact and likelihood of the Risk Profiles are ranked in a scale of 1-10.
The Map report shown above can be used to better understand the risks involved in the organization and plan strategies to mitigate and avoid the the occurrence of risks.
